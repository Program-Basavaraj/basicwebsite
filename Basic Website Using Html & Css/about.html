    • ABOUT US

    Hombale Films was founded by film producer, Vijay Kirangandur & Chaluve Gowda with the goal of creating compelling content for Kannadigas on both at domestic as well as global scale. Our inaugural film production titled Ninnindhale starring the indomitable Puneet Rajkumar, was the stepping stone in our journey to make quality films that strike a chord with our audience. Weve consistently produced critically acclaimed blockbusters from Raajakumara to KGF: Chapter 1.


    At Hombale, we strive to set benchmarks in the way films are created, marketed, and consumed. We are especially cognizant of the need for on-demand content via. digital platforms and have strategically leveraged streaming services for film and music distribution. Today, Hombale Films has become one of the most sought-after production houses in the country having worked with numerous industry stalwarts
