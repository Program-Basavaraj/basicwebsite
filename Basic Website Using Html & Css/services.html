    Vijay Kiragandur is an Indian film Producer, who has worked predominantly in Kannada movie industry. Vijay has worked in popular movies like Yuva RajKumar - Hombale Films, Salaar.

    Hombale Films was founded by Vijay Kiragandur, an entrepreneur from Bangalore. Hombale Films' first production endeavour was the Kannada film Ninnindale in 2014, starring Puneeth Rajkumar.
